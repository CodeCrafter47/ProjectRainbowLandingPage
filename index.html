Contribute

There are a number of ways you can contribute to Rainbow:

SOURCE CODE


Are you a Java programmer?

Want to contribute to the most ground-breaking project the Minecraft community has ever seen

or just love testing stuff to destruction

then writing long and detailed bug reports about it?

Come get involved on GitHub

PLUGIN MAKER


Are you a Java programmer?

What to make an plugin (aka addon/mod) for Rainbow?

Have a look at our PluginAPI located in the Rainbow.zip

Or look at our Javadocs !

FORUMS


Looking for something more casual?

Somewhere you can hang out with fellow Rainbow users?

Critique new plugin ideas and help people out with Rainbow?

Look no further than the Rainbow Forums.
